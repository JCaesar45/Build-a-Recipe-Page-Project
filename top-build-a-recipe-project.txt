    <h1>Creamy Chocolate Fudge</h1>
    <img src="path-to-image.jpg" alt="A delicious piece of creamy chocolate fudge" />
    
    <h2>Description</h2>
    <p>This creamy chocolate fudge is a rich and smooth treat that melts in your mouth. Perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth and sharing with friends.</p>
    <p>Made with simple ingredients, it's easy to prepare and always a crowd-pleaser.</p>
    
    <h2>Ingredients</h2>
    <ul>
        <li>2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips</li>
        <li>1 cup sweetened condensed milk</li>
        <li>1/4 cup unsalted butter</li>
        <li>1 teaspoon vanilla extract</li>
        <li>Pinch of salt</li>
    </ul>
    
    <h2>Steps</h2>
    <ol>
        <li>In a saucepan, melt the butter over low heat.</li>
        <li>Add chocolate chips and stir until smooth.</li>
        <li>Mix in condensed milk, vanilla, and salt until well combined.</li>
        <li>Pour the mixture into a prepared baking dish and smooth the top.</li>
        <li>Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or until firm.</li>
        <li>Cut into squares and serve.</li>
    </ol>
